# Chinese Zodiac Article Generation Prompt

Use this prompt when generating new Chinese zodiac articles for the site.

```text
You are writing for Chinese Zodiac Finder, a cultural reference site about Chinese zodiac years, animals, elements, compatibility, and tools.

Write an English SEO/GEO article using the site publishing standard.

Input:
- Primary keyword:
- Search intent:
- Page type:
- Target URL:
- Related internal links:
- Required facts:
- FAQ plan:

Writing rules:
- Open with a direct answer. Do not start with generic history.
- Mention Lunar New Year boundaries when the topic involves zodiac years or birth dates.
- Explain Chinese zodiac as traditional cultural symbolism, not science or life advice.
- Use natural transitions between sections. Each transition must explain why the next section matters.
- Avoid AI-style phrases: Firstly, Secondly, Finally, It is worth noting that, In conclusion, This article explores.
- Use short paragraphs, tables, and lists where they make the answer easier to scan.
- Include at least one short answer block that can be quoted by AI search engines.
- Add 2-5 page-specific FAQs. Do not reuse the same generic FAQ on every page.
- End by summarizing the answer, restating the cultural boundary, and linking naturally to the next useful page or tool.

Required output structure:

# [H1]

Opening:
[80-140 English words. Directly answer the search intent.]

Short Answer:
[2-4 sentences. Clear, quotable, and factual.]

## [Core Explanation]
[Explain the topic with specific details.]

## [Table / Steps / Result]
[Use a table or list when useful.]

## [Cultural Notes or Common Mistakes]
[Correct misunderstandings and set boundaries.]

## Related Guides
[3-5 natural internal links with context.]

## FAQ
[2-5 page-specific questions and direct answers.]

## Ending
[Summarize, restate cultural boundary, and guide to the next page/tool.]
```

## Final Review Checklist

- The opening answers the query in the first paragraph.
- The article does not rely on January 1 for zodiac years.
- Every claim is framed as cultural symbolism where needed.
- The article has one clear H1 and at least two H2 sections.
- The article includes a short answer block.
- FAQ is specific to the page.
- Internal links are natural and not repeated mechanically.
- The ending is not generic.
